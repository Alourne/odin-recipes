When Life Gives You Strawberry, You Make Lemonade

You gotta love Strawberry Lemonade, it's such an amazing drink and served at most restaurants for adults and children.

Strawberry Lemonade Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Lemons
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Strawberry Syrup

Directions

  1. Puree the strawberries. Use a blender or food processor to puree the strawberries. Strain them through a fine mseh sieve to remove seeds, if you want.
  2. Make a simple syrup Add sugar and 2 cups of water to a saucepan. Bring to a simmer and stir until sugar is dissolved. Removed from heat and cool to room temperature.
  3. Juice your lemons. Remove from heat and cool to room temperature.
  4. Combine syrup, lemon juice, and pureed strawberries. Stir to combine
  5. Wait patiently.
