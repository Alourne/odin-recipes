The Best Dessert on Earth

You may be wondering what the best dessert on earth is huh? I honestly think it's a brownie.

In this recipe, you wll find a detailed ingredient list and step by step instructions in the

recipe to create the perfect brownie.

Ingredients Needed

  • 1/2 Cup of Butter
  • 1 Cup of white sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 Cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Frosting:

  • 3 Tablespoons of butter, softened
  • 3 Tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 Tablespoon of honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 Cup confectioners' sugar

Directions

  1. Step 1:

    2. Preheat the oven to 350 Degrees F (175 Degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.

  2. Step 2:

    3. Melt 1/2 cup butter in a large saucepan. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and

    1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat into 1/3 cup cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder.

    Spread batter into prepared pan

  3. Step 3:

    4. Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the

    sides of the pan, about 25 to 30 minutes. Let it cool briefly before frosting.

  4. Step 4:

    5. To make the frosting: Combine softened butter, confectioners' sugar, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey

    and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract in a bowl. Stir until smooth. Frost brownies while they are still warm.
